After being in the field for 15 years and being an independent consultant for 5 years, I shared my ideas on how to manage your career to hit a significant financial milestone in 5 years. I met and became friends with Cory House at this event, which he journaled here on Cory House’s BitNative Blog. In this blog, Cory mentioned his upcoming “Outliers” talk that he worked on. This was a revolutionary moment for Cory.

Cory’s Outlier Talk, based on Malcolm Gladwell’s book of the same name, has resonated with tens of thousands of people around the world.
I highly recommend taking the time to explore.
