Dustin Thostenson will present "Sphere of Control: Techniques to Tackle Tech Debt Today" at Iowa Code Camp Fall 2026 on November 7, 2026.

Between aggressive deadlines and mounting tech debt, teams often feel trapped by the accumulated complexity of their systems. This session provides practical, proven techniques for tackling tech debt within your sphere of control—the things you can actually influence and change.

Learn technical, political, and procedural strategies to break free from tech debt without requiring a dramatic organizational restructuring. Discover how your team can solve this today and create more joy in new work with less pain from the old.

Register at [Iowa Code Camp](https://iowacodecamp.com/schedule/).
